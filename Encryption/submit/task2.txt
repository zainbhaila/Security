1. The CBC image appears to be a random assortment of colorful pixels. 
The ECB image has the outline of the original image visible. It also repeats the outline a few 
times. And it also has vertical bars of uniform color across a lot of the image with some areas 
filled with assorted pixels.

2. The two methods of encryption encrypt the image in different ways. ECB encrypts all blocks 
individually using the same key and block. CBC encryption relies on the previous blocks to encrypt 
the current block. 

3. CBC does not give any useful info.
ECB gives the outline of the original image.
